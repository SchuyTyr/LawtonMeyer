About


Lawton Meyer is a cinematographer based in the Hudson Valley, New York. His love of stories and creativity was present from a young age. In 2014 he shot his first professional short film “For Our Sins”, a tale of revenge set in Africa, which was accepted into the Cannes International Film Festival. Since then he has worked on a wide variety of productions, including music videos, TV commercials, documentaries, short films, web series, fashion, nature, sports, and more.

He brings a unique vision and passion to every project, and strives to bring stories to life.
